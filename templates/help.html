Search Help & Guide

Learn how to use the IR Legal Search Tool effectively

Search Types

Phrase Search

Search for exact phrases using quotation marks

Example:
"contract law"
"information system"
"legal framework"
Boolean Search

Combine terms using AND, OR, NOT operators

Examples:
contract AND liability
data OR information
copyright NOT trademark
Simple Search

Search for individual words or terms

Examples:
contract
liability
copyright

Search Tips

Do's
  • Use quotes for exact phrases
  • Combine terms with AND for precise results
  • Use OR to broaden your search
  • Try different synonyms
  • Use NOT to exclude unwanted terms
Don'ts
  • Don't use too many operators in one search
  • Don't search for very common words alone
  • Don't forget quotation marks for phrases
  • Don't use special characters unnecessarily
  • Don't make queries too complex

Troubleshooting

  • Check your spelling
  • Try broader or simpler terms
  • Use synonyms or alternative terms
  • Remove quotes to search for individual words
  • Ensure your documents contain the terms you're searching for

  • Use more specific search terms
  • Avoid searching for very common words
  • Use boolean operators to narrow results
  • Consider the size and number of PDF files

  • Make sure operators are in UPPERCASE (AND, OR, NOT)
  • Use spaces around operators: word AND word
  • Don't mix quotes with boolean operators
  • Start with simple boolean queries
